How it works:

  1. Enter your Client ID from the Spotify Developer Dashboard
  2. Add the displayed Redirect URI to your app settings in the Spotify Dashboard
  3. Select the scopes (permissions) your app needs
  4. Click "Start Authorization Flow" to begin the OAuth process
  5. After authorization, you'll be automatically redirected back with your token
  6. Use the extracted token in your application

Note: Access tokens typically expire after 1 hour. Use the "Get New Token" button when you need a fresh one.
